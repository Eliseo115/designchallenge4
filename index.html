The inspiration for Katherine Rusnak is the pursuit of academic excellence and a determination that is wrapped by a neon light. The color is to portray a hopeful luminescence shining from behind the obscurity of shadows.

Color Palette

Color is integral to a brand identity. The cohisiveness of the brand is only reinforced by the color palette. Color serves as a psychological purpose by communicating a certain feeling to your audience.

Though strong background colors like black and grey are used, the aqua tone conveys a cool and calming attribute. The use of aqua can be a symbol for energy, wisdom, and creativity.
